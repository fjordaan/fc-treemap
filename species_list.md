# Fulham Cemetery — Tree Species List

## Acer (maple and sycamore)
Acer negundo, Acer platanoides, Acer platanoides 'Globosum', Acer platanoides 'Schwedleri', Acer pseudoplatanus, Acer saccharinum

## Ash
Fraxinus excelsior, Fraxinus excelsior 'Pendula'

## Cedar
- True cedars: Cedrus atlantica 'Glauca', Cedrus deodara
- Other: Cryptomeria japonica, Thuja plicata

## Cherry
Prunus avium, Prunus avium 'Plena', Prunus 'Beni-yutaka', Prunus cerasifera 'Nigra', Prunus 'Kanzan', Prunus 'Shirotae', Prunus subhirtella 'Autumnalis', Prunus 'Tai Haku', Prunus 'Ukon', Prunus x hillieri 'Spire', Prunus x yedoensis

## Chestnut
Aesculus hippocastanum, Aesculus indica

## Crabapple
Malus floribunda, Malus halliana, Malus sylvestris, Malus trilobata, Malus x purpurea

## Cypress
Chamaecyparis lawsoniana, Chamaecyparis lawsoniana 'Elwoodii', Cupressus sempervirens, Hesperocyparis lusitanica, x Hesperotropsis leylandii

## Hawthorn
Crataegus laevigata 'Paul's Scarlet', Crataegus monogyna 'Pendula'

## Magnolia
Magnolia grandiflora, Magnolia kobus, Magnolia x soulangeana

## Oak
Quercus ilex, Quercus robur

## Pine
Pinus muricata, Pinus pinaster, Pinus sylvestris, Pinus wallichiana

## Plum
Prunus cerasifera 'Nigra', Prunus cerasifera 'Pissardi'

## Poplar
Populus nigra 'Plantierensis', Populus x canadensis 'Serotina Aurea'
